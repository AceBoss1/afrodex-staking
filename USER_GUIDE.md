## AfroX Staking Dashboard

### Overview Cards

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Wallet Balance** | AfroX tokens available in your wallet |
| **Staked Balance** | AfroX tokens currently staked |
| **Accumulated Rewards** | Rewards earned (auto-claimed on unstake) |
| **Badge Tier** | Your current tier based on staked amount |

### How to Stake

1. **Approve** (first time only):
   - Enter the amount you want to stake
   - Click **"Approve"** button
   - Confirm the transaction in your wallet
   - Wait for confirmation

2. **Stake**:
   - Enter the amount to stake
   - Click **"Stake"** button
   - Confirm the transaction
   - Your staked balance will update after confirmation

> **Tip:** Click "MAX" to stake your entire wallet balance

### How to Unstake

1. Enter the amount to unstake
2. Click **"Unstake"** button
3. Confirm the transaction
4. Rewards are automatically claimed when you unstake

> **Note:** Your proxy auto-claims rewards on stake/unstake. To manually trigger claim without a separate claim function, stake/unstake a tiny amount (e.g., 0.0001 AfroX).

### Reward Rates

| Period | Base Rate | Bonus (after 30 days) |
|--------|-----------|----------------------|
| Daily | 0.6% | +0.06% |
| Monthly | ~18% | +~1.8% |
| Yearly | ~219% | +~21.9% |

> **Note:** Reward projections are estimates based on normal chain conditions. Real-time rewards are displayed in the "Accumulated Rewards" card.

## LP Token Lock-Mining Dashboard

### What is LP Mining?

LP Mining allows you to lock your Liquidity Provider (LP) tokens to earn additional rewards. The longer you lock, the higher your APY.

### Lock Duration Options

| Duration | Base APY | Instant Bonus |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| 30 Days | 10% | 5% |
| 60 Days | 17% | 5% |
| 90 Days | 25% | 5% |
| 180 Days | 72% | 5% |
| 365 Days | 155% | 5% |

### How to Lock LP Tokens

1. First, add liquidity in AfroSwap (AfroX + ETH pair)
2. Navigate to **LP Token Lock-Mining Dashboard**
3. Select your lock duration
4. Enter the amount of LP tokens to lock
5. Click **"Lock & Mine"**
6. Confirm the transaction

### Rewards Distribution

- **Instant Bonus (5%)**: Credited immediately upon locking
- **Mining Rewards**: Distributed daily based on APY
- **Referrer Bonus (5%)**: If you used a referral link, your referrer gets 5%

### Early Unlock Penalty

If you unlock before your lock period ends:
- **50% penalty** on remaining rewards
- Instant bonus is not affected

## AfroSwap

### Swap Tokens

1. Click **"AfroSwap"** button
2. Select the **Swap** tab
3. Choose your input token (e.g., ETH)
4. Choose output token (e.g., AfroX)
5. Enter the amount
6. Review the exchange rate and slippage
7. Click **"Swap"**
8. Confirm in your wallet

### Limit Orders

1. Select the **Limit** tab
2. Choose buy or sell
3. Set your target price
4. Enter the amount
5. Set expiry (1h, 24h, 7d, 30d)
6. Click **"Place Order"**

> **Note:** Limit orders are monitored by our price oracle service and executed when your target price is reached.

### Add Liquidity

1. Select the **Pool** tab
2. Enter amounts for both tokens (AfroX + ETH)
3. Review the pool share you'll receive
4. Click **"Add Liquidity"**
5. Confirm in your wallet

> **Tip:** After adding liquidity, you can lock your LP tokens in the LP Mining Dashboard for extra rewards!

## FAQ

### General

**Q: What network does AfroX DeFi Hub use?**
A: Ethereum Mainnet

**Q: Are there any fees?**
A: Only standard Ethereum gas fees. No platform fees for staking.

**Q: Is my wallet safe?**
A: Yes, we never have access to your private keys. All transactions require your approval.

### Staking

**Q: When do I receive my rewards?**
A: Rewards accumulate continuously and are auto-claimed when you unstake.

**Q: Can I stake more while already staking?**
A: Yes! You can add to your stake at any time.

**Q: What happens if I unstake partially?**
A: You receive proportional rewards for the amount unstaked.

### LP Mining

**Q: Where do I get LP tokens?**
A: Add liquidity in AfroSwap (AfroX + ETH) to receive LP tokens.

**Q: Can I unlock early?**
A: Yes, but you'll lose 50% of your remaining rewards.

### Ambassador

**Q: Why is my commission pending?**
A: Commissions have a 30-day lock to ensure referees maintain their stake.

**Q: What if my referral unstakes early?**
A: Your commission for that referral is forfeited.

## Troubleshooting

### Transaction Failed

1. Check your ETH balance for gas
2. Increase gas limit in your wallet
3. Try again with higher slippage (for swaps)

### Wallet Not Connecting

1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Ensure you're on Ethereum Mainnet

### Rewards Not Showing

1. Wait for block confirmations
2. Refresh the page
3. Check your staking transaction was confirmed
